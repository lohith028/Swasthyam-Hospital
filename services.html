Medical Excellence

Our Speciality Services

Comprehensive healthcare solutions under one roof, delivered by experts with cutting-edge technology.

🚨

Emergency & Trauma Surgery

24-hour trauma surgical services with in-house consultants. We are prepared for any medical emergency, accident, or critical surgical intervention at any time of day.

🫀

ICU & Critical Care

Our Intensive Care Unit is equipped with high-end ventilators, multipara monitors, and 24/7 specialist coverage to manage life-threatening conditions.

👩‍⚕️

Women's Health Care

Expert gynaecological and obstetric care, including prenatal care, normal and painless deliveries, and advanced gynaecological surgeries.

🩺

Master Health Check-Up

Tailored preventive health packages for early detection of lifestyle diseases, heart conditions, and general wellness assessment.

🚑

Ambulance Services

Fast-response ambulance fleet equipped with basic and advanced life support systems, ensuring safe transit to the hospital.

💊

In-House Pharmacy & Lab

Full-service pharmacy and diagnostic laboratory for immediate medication and test results, saving precious time during treatment.

🦴

Orthopaedics & Joint Care

Specialized treatment for bone injuries, joint replacements, and sports medicine, backed by a dedicated rehabilitation team.

🧠

Neurology & Neurosurgery

Advanced care for brain and spine disorders, including trauma management and neurological rehabilitation.

💉

Internal Medicine

Expert diagnosis and management of chronic conditions like diabetes, hypertension, and infectious diseases.
