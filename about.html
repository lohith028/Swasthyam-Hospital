Our Story

Dedicated to Your Wellness

Since our inception, Swasthyam Hospital has been committed to providing compassionate and high-quality medical care to the community.

Est.
Bengaluru

Built on Trust, Driven by Care

Swasthyam Super Speciality Hospital was founded with a single mission: to make world-class healthcare accessible to every family in Bengaluru. Located in Herohalli, Annapurneshwari Nagar, we have grown from a community clinic into a trusted multispeciality hospital serving thousands of patients across the city.

Our multidisciplinary team — comprising surgeons, physicians, gynaecologists, intensivists, and paramedics — works together every day to deliver precise diagnosis, compassionate treatment, and full recovery support. From a free multispeciality health camp serving the neighbourhood to complex trauma surgeries, we believe no patient should be left without expert care.

"We believe every patient deserves not just treatment, but genuine care, clear communication, and the confidence that they are in the safest hands."
