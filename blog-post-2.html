How to Get Glowing Skin: 4 Expert Tips for a Radiant Glow

A healthy, radiant glow is often a sign of both good internal health and a consistent external skincare routine. Achieving "glass skin" doesn't require a dozen products; it requires the right balance of natural care and medicinal science. At Swasthyam Super Speciality Hospital, our dermatology team emphasizes a holistic approach to skin wellness.

Here are 4 expert-recommended tips combining the best of nature and medicine.

1. Hydration & Antioxidants (Natural)

Glowing skin starts from within. Water is essential for flushing out toxins and maintaining skin elasticity. Complement your water intake with natural antioxidants:

  • Green Tea: Rich in polyphenols that protect skin from environmental damage.
  • Vitamin C Foods: Oranges, lemons, and amla boost collagen production naturally.
  • Berries: High in antioxidants that fight free radicals, preventing dullness.

2. Sun Protection & Vitamin C Serums (Medicinal)

While the sun provides Vitamin D, overexposure is the #1 cause of skin dullness and ageing. Medicinal intervention is key here:

  • Broad-Spectrum Sunscreen: Use an SPF 30+ daily, even indoors, to prevent pigmentation.
  • Stabilized Vitamin C Serum: A medicinal-grade serum applied in the morning can brighten the complexion and fade dark spots faster than topical fruits ever could.

3. Gentle Exfoliation (Natural)

Dead skin cells act as a barrier to light, making your face look tired. You can clear them away using gentle natural ingredients twice a week:

  • Honey & Sugar: A mild humectant scrub that exfoliates without stripping moisture.
  • Oatmeal Mask: Perfect for sensitive skin, it calms inflammation while removing impurities.

4. Retinoids & Targeted Moisturization (Medicinal)

For long-term radiance and cell turnover, dermatologists often recommend medicinal creams:

  • Retinoids/Retinol: These Vitamin A derivatives are the gold standard for skin renewal and texture improvement.
  • Hyaluronic Acid: A medicinal ingredient that can hold 1000x its weight in water, giving your skin that plump, "bouncy" look instantly.

Conclusion

Consistency is more important than complexity. By combining simple natural habits with targeted medicinal products, you can achieve a lasting glow. Always patch-test new products and consult a professional for chronic skin concerns.
